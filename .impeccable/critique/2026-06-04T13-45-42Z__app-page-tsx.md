## Overall Impression

The engineering ambition is real (the GSAP warp-dive, the 3D core, live socket telemetry) and the brand register rewards ambition. But the surface is wearing the generic "AI SaaS" costume: rainbow-gradient headlines, purple everything, Sparkles-and-stats. The biggest opportunity: keep the ambitious motion, strip the AI-default skin, and commit to one distinctive brand color story (the dashboard already defines a violet brand token system the landing should adopt instead of hardcoding).

## What's Working

1. **The warp-dive choreography** ‚Äî one orchestrated page-load/scroll moment instead of scattered fade-ins. This is exactly the brand-register permission to be ambitious.
2. **Honest real-time telemetry** ‚Äî the socket-driven live visitor count is a real signal (and the right contrast to the fake widget stats).
3. **Thai i18n care** ‚Äî the IME composition guard on Enter prevents submitting a partial Thai syllable. A real detail most miss.

## Priority Issues

- **[P1] Gradient text on the hero H1 and every section heading.** Absolute ban; the loudest AI tell, on the most important element. **Fix:** solid color (foreground or the brand violet token), emphasis through weight/size, drop `bg-clip-text`. Suggested: `/impeccable typeset`
- **[P1] Indigo‚Üípurple‚Üípink is the entire palette.** The 2026 AI monoculture (18 detector hits). The dashboard now has `--brand`/`--accent`/semantic tokens; the landing hardcodes raw Tailwind indigo/violet/pink. **Fix:** route through brand tokens, commit to one saturated strategy, cut the rainbow. Suggested: `/impeccable colorize` (or `quieter`)
- **[P1] Fake placeholder stats in the hero.** "AI Tasks 42", "Saved 12.5h", hardcoded trending hashtags read as a mockup and erode trust on a real workspace. **Fix:** wire real data or remove the widgets. Suggested: `/impeccable harden`
- **[P2] No reduced-motion path for the warp.** A 400vh scroll-pinned, snap-scrolling, 6√ó zoom warp with GSAP/Framer entrances and no `prefers-reduced-motion` fallback is a motion-sensitivity and scroll-jack risk. **Fix:** gate the warp + entrances on `prefers-reduced-motion`, offer a static reveal. Suggested: `/impeccable animate`
- **[P2] Hero overload.** Badge + telemetry + headline + 2 CTAs + AI prompt + 2 stat widgets + 3D robot compete in one viewport; nothing is clearly dominant. **Fix:** one dominant idea per fold; demote or defer the widgets. Suggested: `/impeccable distill`
